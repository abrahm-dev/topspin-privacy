Common Questions

  • How do I create a club? After signing in, choose "Host," then "Create Your Club."
  • How do I join a club? As a Player, tap "Find a Club" to search and connect.
  • Can I delete my account? Yes — from your home screen, tap "Delete My Account." Your account and profile are removed; match history you were part of stays visible to other players in shared tournaments, since that's shared tournament history rather than your personal data.
